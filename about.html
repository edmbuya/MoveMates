About Move Mates

Your trusted partner for unforgettable Kenyan adventures

Our Story

Move Mates was founded with a simple mission: to showcase the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Kenya through exceptional safari tours and luxury accommodations. We believe that travel should be transformative, and we're dedicated to creating experiences that leave lasting memories.

With years of experience in the tourism industry, our team of local experts knows Kenya inside and out. From the vast savannas of Maasai Mara to the pristine beaches of the coast, we've crafted unique experiences that highlight the very best of our beautiful country.

Why Choose Move Mates?

  • Expert local guides with deep knowledge of Kenya's wildlife and culture
  • Luxury accommodations in prime locations
  • Small group sizes for personalized experiences
  • Commitment to sustainable and responsible tourism
  • 24/7 support throughout your journey

Our Values

Passion

We're passionate about Kenya and sharing its wonders with the world. Every tour is crafted with love and attention to detail.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible tourism that protects Kenya's natural heritage for future generations.

Community

We work closely with local communities to ensure tourism benefits everyone and preserves authentic cultures.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service, from planning to execution.
